Who We Are

The V3SSEL Leadership Initiative is a 501(c)(3) organization that seeks to foster healthy policy discussions between leaders in a diversity of fields and youth in communities around the globe.

Our Model

Through open forum seminar events at high schools, libraries, and other venues around the world, we bring leaders with years of experience in various fields to discuss important policy issues with youth. Our model emphasizes back and forth discussion through question and answer periods as well as extensive polling among youth before and after events.
