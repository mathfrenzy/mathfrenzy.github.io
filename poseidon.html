Project Poseidon

Sponsored by Google and Microsoft, Project Poseidon is a free V3ssel initiative designed to help cultivate public speaking and leadership skills for students in 6th to 8th grade.

STUDENT SIGN-UP

Want to be a volunteer?

We are looking for passionate, responsible, and creative individuals to teach our curriculum about public speaking and leadership skills. We prefer a background in MUN or other debate/speaking/leadership organizations, but applications are open to all.

APPLY TO BE A VOLUNTEER INSTRUCTOR

How It Works

From March 20th until June 5th, volunteer instructors will run a weekly one hour zoom class on Saturdays from either 10:30am EST to 11:30am EST (Session 1) or 1:30pm EST to 2:30pm EST (Session 2).

Lesson plans prepared in advance will allow for smooth instruction, and students are guaranteed to emerge from the course with newfound public speaking and leadership skills. Volunteers earn volunteer hours as well as participation in a credible nonprofit service organization.
